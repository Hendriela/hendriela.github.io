Interests

When I am not in the lab, I enjoy spending time outdoors. I regularly go hiking in the surrounding Swiss Alps and went on hiking trips in Austria, Sweden, Norway and Iceland. Especially the Erasmus+ Semester, during which I spent 3 months at the Abisko Scientific Research Station in the Swedish subarctic tundra, was an incredible experience. Additionally, I am a hobby photographer to capture all the amazing landscapes, sights, northern lights and starry nights I encounter.

As so many of us, I picked up a German staple hobby during the quarantine, sourdough baking. I also enjoy tending to our balcony garden, or playing tennis or volleyball.
